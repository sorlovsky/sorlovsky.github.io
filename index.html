Seattle Airbnb Reviews

Posted by Simon Orlovsky in Posts   

Is their a difference in how men and women rate Airbnb's in Seattle? In this post we're going to be looking at 1500 Airbnb reviews to answer that question.

The data we will be using can be found on Kaggle

The gender guesser library is a module that gives functions that will decide whether a name is female or male. The vaderSentiment analysis is a library that determines whether a body of text is positive or negative in its sentiment. When joined together we can use these modules to see differences in how men and women review Airbnbs.
